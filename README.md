## Guide Overview

The full guide is organized to help you go from first boot to confident day-to-day use:

- **Introduction**  
  What QuietBox 2 is, who it’s for, and how the guide is structured.

- **Setup & First Run**  
  Unboxing, connections, initial power-on, and first-time configuration.

- **System Access & Management**  
  How to connect, administer, and maintain your system reliably.

- **Workloads & Development**  
  Running common workloads, development flows, and practical usage patterns.

- **Troubleshooting & Recovery**  
  Diagnostics, common issues, and recovery paths.

- **Reference**  
  Command, configuration, and technical reference material.
